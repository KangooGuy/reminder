    How this works & limitations

    This app checks your GPS position against your saved reminders while it's open (foreground or recently backgrounded). Neither iOS nor Android currently let installed web apps register true OS-level geofences, so if the app has been fully closed for a long time — especially on iOS — a nearby reminder may not fire until you reopen it. Keep it open in a background tab/app-switcher for the best results.
